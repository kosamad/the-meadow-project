Who Are We?

The Meadow Project aims to rejuvenate our environment and bring the outdoors closer to you. We strive to enrich homes with locally grown, seasonal flowers and oxygenating houseplants, fostering the soothing ambiance of nature indoors.

Our company ethos nurtures a harmonious bond with the local community. Through events and newsletters, we aim to educate and inspire, sharing insights on our work and guiding you in creating your own meadow-inspired haven at home.

We prioritize eco-friendly deliveries, utilising bikes whenever possible and our zero-emissions van, Bertha, when needed. Our commitment ensures that our blooms arrive in pristine condition, whether they're transported to your doorstep or collected from our shop.

Our commitment to sustainability is second to none. The Meadow Project has transformed disused farmland into a vibrant greenbelt that enrich wildlifes and provides flowers to our local community. Visitors are welcome to enjoy our fields, participate in our events.
