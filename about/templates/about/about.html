About Us

The Meadow Project is a team of passionate gardeners onn a mission to transform our local meadow into a vibrant, eco-friendly haven. Using sustainable methods, we enrich the landscape with beautiful, affordable wildflowers and cultivated plants that bring joy to homes throughout our community. Our commitment extends beyond gardening—through engaging outreach programs, informative blog posts and community events, we aim to educate and inspire our neighbours to embrace green practices and cultivate their own green spaces. Join them in celebrating nature and nurturing a more beautiful, environmentally conscious community.

Delivery

Our commitment to sustainability extends to our delivery service. Whenever possible, we use eco-friendly bikes to deliver your orders, minimising our carbon footprint. For larger or less accessible deliveries, we use energy-efficient vans.

Enjoy your purchase with the assurance that it arrives in the greenest way possible!

Delivery info: We deliver bouquets, plants, and gifts to postcodes beginning with BS1 to BS6. For same-day deliveries, please call us at 0117 123 4567 or contact us online with any inquiries. We prioritize eco-friendly delivery methods, using bicycles whenever possible and our green, zero-emission van as a backup.
