About Us

The Meadow Project is a team of passionate gardeners onn a mission to transform our local meadow into a vibrant, eco-friendly haven. Using sustainable methods, we enrich the landscape with beautiful, affordable wildflowers and cultivated plants that bring joy to homes throughout our community. Our commitment extends beyond gardening—through engaging outreach programs, informative blog posts and community events, we aim to educate and inspire our neighbours to embrace green practices and cultivate their own green spaces. Join them in celebrating nature and nurturing a more beautiful, environmentally conscious community.

Delivery

Our commitment to sustainability extends to our delivery service. Whenever possible, we use eco-friendly bikes to deliver your orders, minimising our carbon footprint and promoting a healthier environment. For larger or less accessible deliveries, we use energy-efficient vans, ensuring that every step of our process aligns with our dedication to eco-conscious practices. Enjoy your purchase with the assurance that it arrives in the greenest way possible!

Our Events

Join us at our beautifully maintained, all-weather venue located right on site (free parking). During the summer months, we also host events out in the field, making the most of the great outdoors.

No matter the occasion, refreshments are always available for you to enjoy, even if you're not attending a lunch-specific event.
